Privacy by design

Your keys. Your device.
Full stop.

Draftly is a thin layer between you and the AI provider you already trust. There's no middleman — because there's no Draftly server in the loop.

The promise

No servers.
No tracking.
No catch.

  • Keys stay local. Your API keys live in your browser's storage and are sent only to the provider you chose — never to us.
  • No Draftly servers. Requests go straight from your browser to Claude / OpenAI / Gemini. Nothing routes through a Draftly backend.
  • You own the model. Use the plan you already pay for — or run a local model with Ollama for free, fully offline.
  • Nothing is logged. Drafts and conversations are processed in the moment and never stored by us.
